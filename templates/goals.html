Learning goals


1. Develop a strategy (linear, recycle based, reman based) and stick to it

2. Coordination between roles is crucial, e.g. product and process design

3. In response to external events (disruptions) maximize return volume and reman/recycle

4. Price competition/cannibalization is not bad but to be minimized

5. Supply and demand shaping to balance volumes
